Use FIFA-style UI sound design across the app so the experience feels premium, modern, sporty, and responsive.

Sound direction

We want the interface sounds to feel like a polished football game menu:

clean

crisp

futuristic

minimal

athletic

premium

not cartoonish

not childish

not overly aggressive

The sounds should feel like a mix of:

soft digital clicks

subtle synthetic taps

light whooshes

muted confirmation hits

short menu blips

sleek transition sweeps

Overall sound character

The sound palette should feel:

modern sports-tech

sleek and fast

slightly futuristic

elegant and controlled

satisfying but not distracting

Avoid:

cheesy arcade sounds

loud button smashes

childish pop effects

casino-like noises

overly robotic sci-fi effects

anything comedic

Sound types to add
1. Hover sound

For hovering over buttons, cards, and menu items:

very soft digital tick

light airy blip

almost feather-light

should feel responsive, not repetitive

2. Click / selection sound

For selecting a menu item or pressing a button:

short crisp click

subtle electronic tap

slightly stronger than hover

satisfying and confident

should feel like a premium sports game UI

3. Screen transition sound

For moving between pages, tabs, or panels:

soft whoosh

smooth swipe sound

clean transition sweep

subtle sense of movement and momentum

4. Success / completion sound

For successful saves, submitted forms, or completed steps:

clean uplifting confirmation tone

short layered chime with a soft digital finish

should feel rewarding but restrained

5. Error / warning sound

For invalid form inputs or permission issues:

soft low digital pulse

subtle negative tone

clear but not harsh

6. Profile / card open sound

For opening a player profile or dashboard card:

light tech “pop-in” sound

subtle glassy click with a short whoosh tail

Interaction mapping

Apply sounds to:

homepage menu buttons

academy cards

player profile cards

dashboard navigation

tabs

filters

save buttons

submission buttons

screen transitions

modal open/close

dropdown selection

Sound behaviour rules

keep volume low to medium

sounds must be short

no sound should feel annoying after repeated use

use slight variation if possible so repeated actions do not feel mechanical

support mute on/off in settings

do not autoplay ambient sound

prioritise usability and polish

Design reference

Aim for:
“FIFA / EA Sports-style menu UI sound design with clean synthetic clicks, subtle navigation ticks, polished transition whooshes, and premium confirmation tones.”
