Our Story

We're on a mission to revolutionize children's literature through personalized storytelling technology that connects families and inspires imagination.

Our Mission & Values

At Me Time Stories, we believe in the power of personalized storytelling to create meaningful connections between children and their families while fostering a love for reading.

Personalized Experience

We create stories that adapt to each child, making them the hero of their own adventure and creating a deeper emotional connection to reading.

Educational Growth

Our stories are designed to support developmental milestones, encourage imagination, and build essential literacy skills in an engaging way.

Family Connection

We strengthen bonds between parents and children through shared reading experiences that create lasting memories and meaningful conversations.

Our Journey

From concept to reality, follow the evolution of Me Time Stories as we've grown from a simple idea to a revolutionary platform for personalized children's literature.

2022

Early Development

Started building the technology foundation in stealth mode, designing our modular, open-source architecture for future scalability.

  • Technology foundation initiation
  • Beginning of stealth mode development
  • Start of modular architecture design
2023-2024

Core Development

Focused on building our Dynamic Personalization Framework (DPF), integrating narrative logic with character adaptation systems.

  • DPF (Dynamic Personalization Framework) creation
  • Dashboard development for parents and children
  • Print token system implementation
  • IP security establishment
January 2025

Official Launch

Registered the company and officially exited stealth mode with a library of over 100 personalized stories ready for families.

  • Company registration
  • Exit from stealth mode
  • 100+ story library achievement
February 2025

Current Status

Prototype completed and seeking development funding to prepare the platform for customer use with established printer partnerships.

  • Seeking development funding
  • Platform preparation for customer use
  • Printer partnership establishment

Technology Foundation

Our innovative technology platform combines advanced personalization algorithms with intuitive interfaces to create truly unique storytelling experiences.

Modular Architecture

Our open-source architecture ensures flexibility, cost efficiency, and future scalability as we grow.

  • Component-based design for easy updates
  • Seamless integration capabilities
  • Optimized for performance and reliability

Dynamic Personalization Framework

Our proprietary DPF integrates narrative logic, character adaptation, and data-driven personalization.

  • Advanced narrative branching system
  • Character customization engine
  • Contextual learning algorithms

User Experience Infrastructure

Intuitive dashboards and management systems for both parents and children to customize their experience.

  • Parent/child interactive dashboards
  • Content management system
  • Print token system for physical books

Where We Are Today
