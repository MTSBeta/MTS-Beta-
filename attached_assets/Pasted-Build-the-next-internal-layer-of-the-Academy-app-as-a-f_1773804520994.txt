Build the next internal layer of the Academy app as a full Story Production Workspace.

This is an INTERNAL production tool for Me Time Stories.
It must sit on top of the current academy intake app, current database structure, and current Google Sheets sync.
Do not break the existing player-facing or academy-facing demo flows.
Add this as a new internal area/route only.

PRIMARY GOAL

Turn all submitted player intake data into one clean, human-first creative workspace where authors and internal creators can:
- understand the player quickly
- see all relevant inputs in one place
- build the story inside the app
- manage illustrations and media inside the app
- track production status
- send preview versions to academy admins before final sign-off

CORE PRODUCT RULES

1. Authors must NOT begin with raw form data.
2. All player, staff, stakeholder, image, and voice-note inputs should be unified into one clear creator-facing profile.
3. Database is the source of truth.
4. Google Sheets remains linked as a sync / ops / reporting layer only, not the primary creative workspace.
5. This must remain human-first. The system should guide the author, not replace the author.
6. Preserve creative freedom, but provide structured support and Me Time Stories brand rules.
7. Build for future AI assistance, but do NOT make this a one-click story generator.
8. Keep the current demo app intact and add this as a separate internal production area.

2. PLAYER STORY PROFILE

Create a unified Player Story Profile page.

This should be the first major screen the author sees.
It should combine all relevant inputs into one editorial, elegant, easy-to-read creator brief.
It must NOT feel like a spreadsheet dump or raw database view.

The profile should combine:
- player intake answers
- coach input
- internal staff observations
- parent input if present
- stakeholder responses if present
- uploaded images
- voice notes
- transcripts if available
- approved reference notes

The profile should have these sections:

A. At a Glance
- name
- age band
- academy
- position
- current story status
- assigned author
- assigned illustrator
- completion indicators

B. Identity Summary
- who the player is
- key personality traits
- how they describe themselves
- how they describe their game
- self-image and tone

C. Emotional Profile
- emotional challenge
- pressure pattern
- response to setbacks
- hidden quality
- what keeps them going
- repeated internal tension that may shape the story

D. Football Narrative
- proud moment
- overlooked / disappointment moment
- hard period summary
- turning point summary
- dream / future goal
- key football test

E. Support Network
- first person they tell good news to
- support figures
- who understands them best
- coach voice summary
- parent voice summary
- staff highlights

F. Signature Details
- meaningful object
- rituals / routines
- image-based cues
- appearance notes
- values
- themes
- repeated phrases
- symbolic details

G. Creator Guidance
- best themes to build around
- emotional opportunities
- academy values to embed

- likely story spine

H. Source Materials
- uploaded images
- voice notes
- transcripts
- staff submissions
- stakeholder responses
- raw source drawer (collapsible)

Important:
The default experience must be summarised and grouped.
Add a collapsible raw source drawer for authors who want to verify the original wording.

4. STORY BUILDER

Create a guided story-building interface using this fixed six-part structure:

1. The Vision
2. The Storm
3. Rock Bottom
4. The Rise
5. Elite Wisdom
6. Next Level

For each of the six sections, show:
- section title
- section purpose
- relevant source details
- guided notes
- emotional beat
- football-specific prompts
- academy-value prompts
- what to avoid
- editable manuscript writing area
- optional scene notes area

Suggested section purposes:

The Vision
- introduce the player’s dream, identity, world, and what they want

The Storm
- introduce challenge, pressure, disappointment, or conflict

Rock Bottom
- show the lowest point, fracture, doubt, or hardest test

The Rise
- show support, change, action, or new belief beginning to form

Elite Wisdom
- show the deeper lesson, truth, or wisdom gained through the journey

Next Level
- end with transformation, momentum, and future promise

Important:
The author writes the story.
The system supports structure, not full automation.
