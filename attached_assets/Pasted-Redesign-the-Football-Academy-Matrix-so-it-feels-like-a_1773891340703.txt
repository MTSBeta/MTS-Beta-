Redesign the Football Academy Matrix so it feels like a premium academy-facing proof-of-understanding page, not a basic position selector.

Important context:
This matrix is not just to explain football positions.
Its purpose is to show academies that Me Time Stories deeply understands the psychology, identity, pressures, and developmental journey of each role on the pitch.

This page should help an academy instantly understand:
- we know what each position demands emotionally and mentally
- we know how to build a personalised story around that role
- we make the child the hero of their specific position
- our story system is position-aware, player-aware, and development-aware

So the selected position should not just show generic bullet points.
It should show how that position becomes a hero journey.

What I want:
- make the pitch and position selection feel more polished and interactive
- improve visual hierarchy and selected-state feedback
- when a position is selected, show a richer academy-facing programme view in modular cards, not just a few bullets
- make the whole page feel premium, intelligent, and valuable to academy decision-makers

For each position, structure the content like this:

1. Position Identity
A short statement describing who this player is in the team and what role they carry.

2. Hero Tension
The emotional and psychological tension of that role.
For example: pressure, isolation, visibility, responsibility, fear of mistakes, decision-making burden, etc.

3. Hero Arc
How the player grows through story.
This should show how the child becomes the hero of that role.
Example:
- from self-doubt to courage
- from pressure to composure
- from silence to leadership

4. Story Themes
The main developmental and emotional themes explored in the story.

5. Example Story Angle or Story Title
Show how the role becomes a personalised story.
This should feel vivid and specific.

6. Inputs We Use
Show how the story is built from:
- player voice
- coach insight
- parent contribution
- academy values

7. Programme Deliverables
What the academy receives from this role-based story programme.

8. Clear CTA
For example:
- Request this programme
- View sample story pathway
- Discuss academy pilot

Important:
The page should clearly communicate that each player is not just assigned a position.
They are made the hero of that position in a personalised development story.

Design direction:
- premium
- structured
- football-specific
- modern
- more strategic than emotional
- suitable for academy heads, coaches, and decision-makers
- interactive but polished
- modular cards rather than one block of text

Do not make it feel like a simple placeholder page.
Do not make it feel like a generic coaching dashboard.
Make it feel like a serious story-led player development framework that proves Me Time Stories understands the positional identity of every player.

Also improve the pitch interaction visually:
- cleaner pitch design
- better hover states
- clearer selected role treatment
- stronger focus on the active position
- smooth transitions between roles

The selected position area should feel rich enough that an academy can immediately picture one of their own players as the hero in that role.
